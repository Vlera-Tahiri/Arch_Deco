About Us

“In an ever-changing world, we believe in the power of design to create positive change in living and working spaces for everyone”

Archdeco is an integrated design, architecture, planning and consulting firm. Our team of professionals have over 15 years of experience covering a broad spectrum of industry sectors in Kosovo and around the world. Archdeco provides a diverse range of quality services to clients with one goal in mind: to bring their vision to life.

As such, we pride ourselves in the support we provide in our services from concept to execution. The success of our firm is rooted in the hard work put forth by our team and the referrals made by our satisfield clients.

OUR SERVICES

PLANNING

Our team’s extensive experience in the industry has contributed to successful planning and development of projects and a satisfied clientele.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

We work closely with your team to develop the best ways in which to build the project, maximize efficiency to stay on budget and schedule. Offering and helping to choose sub-contractors that will fit with your goals

QUALITY CONTROL

Quality of services which ArchDeco offers, is guaranteed by an array of controls and is checked from the competent project management personnel.

STAGES OF EXECUTION

Effective collaboration between our team and our clients is a valuable part of the execution of the project in the phases of static accounts of the facility, in the design and implementation of electricity, water and sewerage systems, production of furniture, inventory and aesthetic accessories, hydro and thermal insulation, painting,woodwork, stonework, and ceramics and iron works as well.

CHANGING USE OF BUILDING

Our experience in structure design, renovation and management make us a great choice in developing and epositioning structures to fit both the community and the vision our clients have for their projects.

DESIGN

Archdeco’s strengths lie in the fact that experts in the fields of design, architecture and construction can come together in designs that lead the industry. We believe that the aesthetic design of a project is just as important as it’s functionality. And, while we incorporate new trends and designs from all over the world, we also pride ourselves in incorporating senses that are unique to our country and culture. As such, we have become leaders in cultural, environmental and commercial architecture projects around Kosovo and abroad.
